## Design Notes for Slide Creation

1. **Three columns should have glassmorphic card styling:** Semi-transparent background (`rgba(21, 28, 44, 0.85)`), thin border (`rgba(0, 212, 255, 0.12)`), border-radius 16px.
2. **Each column header** should have its own accent color left-border (3px solid).
3. **Key metrics** (90, 0.78, 45s) should be in JetBrains Mono, slightly larger, in the respective accent color.
4. **Top-right corner:** A small animated green dot with "LIVE" text (for the digital version).
5. **Bottom tech strip:** Full-width, subtle dark background, monospaced text.
6. **No clip art or stock photos.** The slide should feel like a Bloomberg terminal screenshot — all information, no decoration.
